Why IT

My history of creating websites, perhaps to your surprise, begins with baking cakes. When I had a few proven recipes for baking, I wanted to boast about everything good that can be baked. I also thought that my own website could also become my own collection of the best recipes that I can try and learn something new along the way. I created my first website without coding at webwavecms in a website builder. Here are the effects of my work: Prosto_i_ pysznie_J.D. After stories from a friend about the wonderful world of programming and a short introduction to C#, I thought I wanted to enter the world of IT. I want a bigger one possibility of development, learning about new, interesting technologies and more creative work.
On a daily basis I work as a Road Supervision Technologist in Poland. I am also prompted by the nature of my industry, which requires relocation with each new Contract. I like to travel but I also like to have more influence on it. It's great when you have a choice and you can say: I can and don't have to.

Why YouCode

I found the Frontend Developer course thanks to an ad on Facebook. I clicked on the link and watched a video in which an experienced programmer talks in an interesting and encouraging way about programming and javascript. I liked that the course offers full access to the materials for unlimited time. Thanks to this, I can adjust my study time to the time of my current job.
